# Show your avatar in the bottom Profile tab

**What changes**

- The Profile tab in the bottom bar will display your actual profile photo instead of the generic person silhouette.
- When the tab is selected, the avatar gets a subtle teal ring to match the rest of the active tab styling.
- If you haven't uploaded a photo yet, the tab falls back to your colored initials circle (same look used elsewhere in the app), and if even that isn't available, the original silhouette icon is shown.
- The avatar updates automatically after you change your profile picture in Edit Profile — no app restart needed.

**Where you'll see it**

- The bottom tab bar across Home, Train, Community, Discover, and Profile — only the Profile icon changes.
