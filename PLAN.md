# Add Biomarkers tab to Body detail, paginate logs, float back button

## What's changing on the Body detail page

**Two tabs at the top**

- A clean editorial tab switcher under the headline: **Composition** (everything you have today — weigh-ins, measurements, trends, photos, goal) and **Biomarkers** (the full bloodwork overview, including the category strip, status ribbon, marker grid, and "+ add panel" action).
- Switching tabs cross-fades smoothly; the masthead and stat triptych stay above the switcher.
- The standalone Biomarkers entry point in Profile keeps working, but now also lives natively inside the Body page so it feels like one comprehensive body view.

**Paginated history (weight + measurements)**

- Under **Trends & History** and under **Measurements**, the long log lists are minimized by default — only the **10 most recent entries** show.
- A subtle "Show 10 more" pill appears at the bottom, tapping it reveals the next batch. Once everything's visible, the pill becomes "Show less" to collapse back to 10.
- Entry count and total stay visible in the section header so you always know how many are hidden.

**Floating back button**

- The default nav bar is hidden on this page.
- A circular glass back button floats in the top-left, above the editorial banner, with a soft blur backdrop so it stays legible as you scroll over photos, charts, and gradients.
- Sits in the safe area, has a 44pt tap target, and animates a gentle press feedback.
