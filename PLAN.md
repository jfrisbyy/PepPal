# Replace water ring with a filling bottle silhouette

### What changes
- Replace the circular progress ring on the home Water card with a sleek, premium water-bottle silhouette that gradually fills from the bottom as you log water.
- The fill rises smoothly to match your progress toward today's goal, with a gentle animated wave rippling across the water surface.
- The bottle uses the app's blue accent with a soft gradient fill, a subtle highlight on the glass, and a clean cap detail to feel editorial and premium.
- The current amount and unit (ml / oz) sit cleanly next to the bottle, keeping the rest of the card layout (goal, remaining, quick-add buttons) unchanged.
- When you tap a quick-add button, the water level animates upward with a smooth spring; reaching your goal triggers a gentle celebratory shimmer on the surface.
